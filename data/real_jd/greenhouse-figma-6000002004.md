## Job description

Figma is growing our team of passionate creatives and builders on a mission to make design accessible to all. Figma’s platform helps teams bring ideas to life—whether you're brainstorming, creating a prototype, translating designs into code, or iterating with AI. From idea to product, Figma empowers teams to streamline workflows, move faster, and work together in real time from anywhere in the world. If you're excited to shape the future of design and collaboration, join us!
We’re hiring a Brand Producer on the Brand Studio team. You’ll lead high-impact brand initiatives, developing the creative production strategy for your projects, demonstrating operational excellence, and ensuring our brand shines across every touchpoint. This role requires collaboration, clarity, and a passion for both process and people.
As a Brand Producer, you’ll partner with other cross-functional teams to scope, prioritize, and deliver a range of creative initiatives. You’ll provide strategic oversight to ensure that projects align with business goals. You’ll also go deep on building project plans that enable our creative team to do what they do best - focus on the creative.
This is a full-time role, based in one of our US hubs or remotely within the United States.
What you'll do at Figma:
Oversee creative production for growth / paid media (campaigns + ads) and web initiatives, ensuring alignment with the company's vision and objectives
Drive cross-functional collaboration in fast-paced, fluid environments, proactively keeping projects on track and optimizing for resource efficiencies
Develop and implement comprehensive project timelines, identifying critical dependencies and key milestones for feedback and approvals from leadership
Manage lean team resources effectively, including onboarding agencies and freelancers, while ensuring accountability and high-quality execution
Establish scalable workflows and processes, promoting efficiency, creative excellence, and maintaining strong relationships with external creative partners
We’d love to hear from you if you:
10+ years of extensive experience in design operations, creative production, or program management roles
Proven experience in managing complex, multi-faceted projects from start to finish, balancing big-picture goals with day-to-day execution
Thrive in fast-paced environments and are adept at bringing structure and clarity to ambiguity
Highly organized, with expertise in developing timelines, identifying critical dependencies, and driving project efficiency
While not required, it’s an added plus if you also have:
Proficient in resource management within lean team structures, including managing external partners like agencies and freelancers
Proficient in Figma and Asana
At Figma, one of our values is Grow as you go. We believe in hiring smart, curious people who are excited to learn and develop their skills. If you’re excited about this role but your past experience doesn’t align perfectly with the points outlined in the job description, we encourage you to apply anyways. You may be just the right candidate for this or other roles.
