About Us

Today the company runs one of the world’s largest networks that powers millions of websites and other Internet properties for customers ranging from individual bloggers to SMBs to Fortune 500 companies. Cloudflare protects and accelerates any Internet application online without adding hardware, installing software, or changing a line of code. Internet properties powered by Cloudflare all have web traffic routed through its intelligent global network, which gets smarter with every request. As a result, they see significant improvement in performance and a decrease in spam and other attacks. Cloudflare was named to Entrepreneur Magazine’s Top Company Cultures list and ranked among the World’s Most Innovative Companies by Fast Company.

If you’re the type of person who values curiosity over bureaucracy, and that AI is a partner in solving tough problems to keep the Internet moving forward, you’ll fit right in.

About the Role
As a Business Analyst Intern on our Marketing team, you will play a crucial role in transforming data into actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making. You will dive into our Go-to-Market (GTM) data, identifying key trends, opportunities for improvement, and potential challenges. Your work will involve building compelling data visualizations and dashboards (using tools like Tableau or PowerBI) to help leadership understand performance across various regions and initiatives. This internship offers a unique opportunity to apply analytical skills in a fast-paced, high-impact environment, directly contributing to Cloudflare's mission and growth.
Responsibilities
Analyze Cloudflare's Go-to-Market (GTM) data to identify performance trends, such as regional variations or campaign effectiveness.
Develop and maintain interactive data visualizations and dashboards using tools like Tableau or PowerBI to present findings clearly and concisely to leadership.
AI Workflow Innovation: Partner with the team to develop and refine custom Gemini "Gems" (specialized AI agents) designed to automate data cleaning, routine reporting, and regional content analysis
Collaborate with team members to understand business questions and translate them into analytical problems.
Support data-driven decision-making by providing timely and accurate insights into marketing performance and business operations.
Contribute to the continuous improvement of data collection, analysis, and reporting processes.
Skills & Qualifications
Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Economics, Finance, Data Analytics, Business Administration, or a related quantitative field.
Demonstrated proficiency in Microsoft Excel, including advanced functions such as Pivot Tables and VLOOKUPs.
Prompt Engineering & AI Orchestration: Familiarity with LLMs - specifically Gemini - with a demonstrated ability to build custom "Gems" or structured prompts that automate data synthesis and content generation
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with an ability to interpret complex datasets.
Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability to articulate findings to both technical and non-technical audiences.
Curiosity and a proactive approach to learning new tools and methodologies.
Available for a full-time 20-week (minimum 12 week) internship, working from our Singapore office 3-5 days a week from July - December.
Bonus Points
Familiarity with SQL or Python for data extraction and manipulation.
Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or PowerBI.
Prior internship experience in a data analysis or business intelligence role.
