## Job description

Datadog is seeking a highly organized and execution-focused
Associate Field Marketing Manager
to support the West and Central NAMER region. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys event logistics, thrives in a fast-paced environment, and is passionate about delivering seamless client-facing experiences.
As part of the NAMER Field Marketing team, you will own the execution and operational delivery of regional programs, including hospitality events, third-party sponsorships, vendor coordination, and scalable initiatives such as event-in-a-box programs for emerging products. This role is critical in supporting pipeline-generating programs while enabling Field Marketing Managers to focus on strategic planning and product-aligned campaigns.
At Datadog, we value our office culture - the relationships and collaboration it builds and the creativity it brings. We operate as a hybrid workplace to help our Datadogs create work-life harmony that fits them best.
What You’ll Do
Support pipeline driving activities for target accounts, ensuring smooth delivery from planning through post-event follow-up
Coordinate hospitality events, executive dinners, third-party sponsorships, regional activations, and local programs
Manage vendors, contracts, shipping, inventory, on-site logistics, and budget tracking
Support scalable initiatives such as event-in-a-box kits and repeatable regional playbooks
Partner closely with Sales and Field Marketing Managers to align programs with territory priorities
Assist with campaign setup, tracking, and reporting in marketing and sales systems
Maintain documentation and help build processes that improve efficiency
Support execution of ancillary events, onsite meeting programs and larger Datadog programs including DASH, Summits, Datadog Live, and industry events like re:Invent, Google Cloud Next, RSA
Who You Are
1–2 years of experience in field marketing, event marketing, event coordination, or program support
Highly organized with strong attention to detail and time management skills
Comfortable managing multiple projects and deadlines in a fast-paced environment
Proactive and dependable, with a strong sense of ownership over execution
Clear communicator who collaborates well across Sales, Marketing, and cross-functional teams
Process-oriented and interested in building scalable systems
Familiarity with marketing or sales tools (e.g., SFDC, Marketo) is a plus
Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to present ideas thoughtfully and clearly to influence and collaborate effectively across all levels of the organization
Ability to travel up to 50% of the time
Datadog values people from all walks of life. We understand not everyone will meet every qualification on day one — and that’s okay. If you’re excited about technology and eager to grow your career in field marketing, we encourage you to apply.
