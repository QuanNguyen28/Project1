- **Company:** Datadog

- **Employment type:** Not specified

## Job description
As an Account Executive (AE) on our Commercial Sales team, you will assist in Datadog’s overall business growth by strategically engaging and closing net-new customers across Commercial businesses (1-999 employees).
Sellers follow a well-defined methodology, collaborate with internal stakeholders, identify the customer's unique needs, and clearly convey the value of the Datadog product.
AEs have the opportunity to grow their careers in Sales and continue contributing to Datadog team success.
This is a hunter sales role where you’ll be responsible for the entire Sales process.
At Datadog, we place value in our office culture - the relationships and collaboration it builds and the creativity it brings to the table.
We operate as a hybrid workplace to ensure our Datadogs can create a work-life harmony that best fits them.
