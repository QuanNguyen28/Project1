## Job description
Datadog is seeking a highly organized and execution-focused
Associate Field Marketing Manager
to support the West and Central NAMER region.
This role is ideal for someone who enjoys event logistics, thrives in a fast-paced environment, and is passionate about delivering seamless client-facing experiences.
As part of the NAMER Field Marketing team, you will own the execution and operational delivery of regional programs, including hospitality events, third-party sponsorships, vendor coordination, and scalable initiatives such as event-in-a-box programs for emerging products.
This role is critical in supporting pipeline-generating programs while enabling Field Marketing Managers to focus on strategic planning and product-aligned campaigns.
At Datadog, we value our office culture - the relationships and collaboration it builds and the creativity it brings.
