CRE is SRE applied outward, the same engineering discipline, applied to the reliability of the systems our customers run on Cloudflare.

The Role
CRE is a rapid response team and a proactive engineering team.
You fix things at the edge as they come up, and you help build the product capabilities that identify customer issues before they become a crisis.
Both modes are equally core.
Rapid response.

You reproduce the defect, isolate the root cause across Cloudflare's infrastructure and the customer's stack, drive the fix with Product Engineering, and confirm resolution.
