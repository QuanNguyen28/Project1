## Job description
As a Research Engineer on our team, you will partner with Research Scientists to turn research ideas into working systems, building the data, tooling, and infrastructure that enable rapid iteration, trustworthy evaluation, and a smooth path from prototype to production.
Building on our track record of AI-powered solutions (e.g.,
Bits AI
,
Bits Evolve
, and our
time series foundation model
), Datadog AI Research tackles high-risk, high-reward problems grounded in real-world challenges in cloud observability and security.
We are focused on two research areas:
World Models for Observability
-- Training multimodal foundation models that learn the joint dynamics of distributed systems across metrics, traces, logs, topology, and events.
These models power advanced forecasting, anomaly detection, root cause analysis, counterfactual simulation ("what if?"), and provide a learned planning backbone for our autonomous agents.
