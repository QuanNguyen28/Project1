# Analyst Relations Manager
- **Company:** Datadog
- **Location:** New York, New York, USA; San Francisco, California, USA
- **Employment type:** Not specified
- **Original posting:** https://careers.datadoghq.com/detail/8003021/?gh_jid=8003021
## Job description
Datadog is seeking an Analyst Relations Manager to help drive Datadog’s Analyst Relations efforts across AI and emerging technology markets.
In this role, you will drive strategic engagement with industry analysts covering AI, cloud platforms, developer tools, security, infrastructure, and adjacent technologies, ensuring they have a clear understanding of Datadog’s platform, innovation, and long-term vision.
You will partner closely with Product Management, Product Marketing, and senior leadership to translate complex technical capabilities into compelling narratives that strengthen Datadog’s position across the evolving AI ecosystem.
This role will help build and scale Datadog’s analyst engagement strategy across AI-related markets and support cohesive AI storytelling across the company’s broad product portfolio.
