The
Associate Designer Program (ADP)
is a 12-month rotational program built for speed, growth, and learning.
You’ll move through three design rotations, each focused on solving high-impact, real-world challenges across our product ecosystem.
You'll design, test, ship, and learn at pace.
You’ll collaborate directly with product managers, engineers, and senior designers, guided by dedicated mentors who push your craft and strategic thinking.
By the end of the program, you’ll have developed diverse knowledge of Datadog’s portfolio and built the skills to design confidently in a fast-moving, global environment.
